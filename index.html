Facilities in College


Lush Green campus and airy rooms

Hostel capmus is lush green with many tress and plant which makes the campus environment fresh and airy. Also the rooms are big,airy and your room will have all the furniture and facilities you need for a comfortable stay.

Mess and Food

Hostel mess delivers a clean, tasty and healthy dining experience. All menu items are decided along with students. Our menu contains wide variety of foods which are tasty and healthy. We use quality range of edile products sourced locally and are always fresh and hygenic.

Sports facilities

Excellent indoor and outdoor facilities are available on hostel campus for organizing of regular sports team practices, and recreational activities for students. Galgotias has sports facilities for Football, Cricket, Badminton, Volleyball, Athletics and Table Tennis.
